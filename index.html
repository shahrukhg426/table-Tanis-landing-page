Who We Are

Questions

GOT QUESTIONS? WE'VE GOT ANSWERS.

Do I need prior experience to join? ▼
Not at all! Our club welcomes players of all levels, from complete beginners to advanced competitors. We offer coaching and practice sessions tailored to different skill levels so everyone can improve and enjoy the game.
What equipment do I need to bring? ▼
If you have your own paddle, feel free to bring it. However, we also provide paddles and balls for beginners, so you can start playing without any special equipment.
When and how often do you meet? ▼
We hold regular sessions at Harris Girls Academy SE22. Sessions run from 1pm to 4pm. Contact us on WhatsApp for the latest schedule and to book your place.
Is the club open to juniors and adults? ▼
Yes! We offer sessions for juniors (from age 6), adults, and seniors. Our goal is to create a fun, inclusive environment where players of all ages can learn and compete.
How do I book a coaching session? ▼
You can book directly via our online form at the "Book Now" button, message us on WhatsApp at +447821763379, or use the contact form on this page. We'll get back to you quickly!
Are there tournaments and competitions? ▼
Yes! We run monthly tournaments, a junior league, and a club championship. Members also get support and guidance for entering external competitions and national events.

SAFEGUARDING POLICY

Our Commitment

Dulwich Table Tennis Club is fully committed to the safety and welfare of all children and young people in our care. The wellbeing of every player is our highest priority.

DBS Checks

All coaches and staff who work with children at DTTC hold a valid Enhanced DBS (Disclosure and Barring Service) check, renewed regularly in line with best practice guidelines.

Code of Conduct for Coaches

  • Coaches never work alone with a child in a closed, private setting
  • Physical contact is limited to what is necessary for instruction
  • All coaches are trained in safeguarding awareness and first aid
  • Any concerns are reported immediately to the Club Welfare Officer

Reporting Concerns

If you have any concerns about the welfare or safety of a child at DTTC, contact us at info@dulwichttc.com or call +44 7821 763379. For urgent concerns: NSPCC helpline 0808 800 5000.

Review

This safeguarding policy is reviewed annually. Last reviewed: January 2026.
