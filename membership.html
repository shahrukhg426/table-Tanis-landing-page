Questions

GOT QUESTIONS? WE'VE GOT ANSWERS.

Do I need prior experience to join? ▼
Not at all! Dulwich TTC welcomes complete beginners through to advanced competitors. Our coaches will make sure you feel at home from your very first session.
What equipment do I need to bring? ▼
Nothing — we have rackets available at the club for all players. If you have your own paddle you're welcome to bring it, but it's certainly not required to get started.
When and how often do you meet? ▼
We run sessions multiple times per week. Check our contact page or WhatsApp us for the latest schedule and to find a session that suits you.
Is the club open to juniors and adults? ▼
Yes! We welcome players aged 6 and above. We have dedicated junior coaching sessions as well as adult and mixed open sessions throughout the week.
How do I book a coaching session? ▼
Simply top up your club account and pay for your session online. You can also contact us via WhatsApp or email to enquire about coaching availability.
Are there tournaments and competitions? ▼
Absolutely! We run monthly tournaments, a junior league, and a club championship each year. We also arrange social visits to other clubs across South London.

PRIVACY POLICY

Who We Are

Dulwich Table Tennis Club ("DTTC", "we", "us", "our") is a sports club based at Harris Girls Academy, SE22 0NR, South London. Company Number: 16745371. We are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

  • Full name, email address, and phone number
  • Date of birth (for junior members and competition registration)
  • Emergency contact details
  • Payment information (processed securely via third-party providers)
  • Photographs taken at club sessions or events (with consent)

How We Use Your Information

  • Process your membership and session bookings
  • Communicate important club news, schedule changes, and updates
  • Register you or your child for competitions and leagues

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as you remain an active member or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at info@dulwichttc.com.

SAFEGUARDING POLICY

Our Commitment

Dulwich Table Tennis Club is fully committed to the safety and welfare of all children and young people in our care. The wellbeing of every player is our highest priority.

DBS Checks

All coaches and staff who work with children at DTTC hold a valid Enhanced DBS check, renewed regularly in line with best practice guidelines.

Code of Conduct for Coaches

  • Coaches never work alone with a child in a closed, private setting
  • Physical contact is limited to what is necessary for instruction
  • All coaches are trained in safeguarding awareness and first aid
  • Any concerns are reported immediately to the Club Welfare Officer

Reporting Concerns

If you have any concerns about the welfare or safety of a child at DTTC, contact us at info@dulwichttc.com or call +44 7821 763379. For urgent concerns: NSPCC helpline 0808 800 5000.

Review

This safeguarding policy is reviewed annually. Last reviewed: January 2026.
