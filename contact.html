Last updated: January 2025

1. Who We Are

Dulwich Table Tennis Club ("we", "us", "our") is a registered community sports club operating across South London. You can contact us at: info@dulwichttc.com or +447821763379.

2. What Data We Collect

We may collect your name, phone number, email address, and details of your enquiry when you use our website or contact us. We do not collect sensitive data or payment card details.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to respond to your enquiry, process memberships, schedule sessions, and communicate about bookings. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Legal Basis

We process your data on the basis of your consent when you submit our contact form, and legitimate interests in running our club.

5. Retention

We retain contact details and membership records for up to 3 years for administrative purposes, after which they are securely deleted.

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. To exercise these rights, contact us at info@dulwichttc.com.

Safeguarding Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Our Commitment

Dulwich Table Tennis Club is committed to providing a safe and enjoyable environment for all participants, especially children and young people. The welfare of all members is our highest priority.

DBS Checks

All coaches and volunteers working with children hold current DBS (Disclosure and Barring Service) checks. We are 100% DBS checked across our coaching team.

Code of Conduct

All coaches, volunteers, and members are expected to adhere to our Code of Conduct, which promotes respect, inclusion, and positive behaviour at all times.

Reporting Concerns

If you have any safeguarding concerns, please contact our Welfare Officer immediately at info@dulwichttc.com or call +447821763379. All concerns will be taken seriously and handled confidentially.

Photography & Media

We require written consent before photographing or recording any participant under the age of 18. Images are never shared publicly without explicit consent.
